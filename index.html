Simon Game
Press any key to start.

  1. Right upon starting, a random button will flash; press that button.

  2. If you pressed on a screen button to start instead, there may be 1 or 2 flashes. If there is 1, press on your first button and then the one that flashed. If there are 2, pressed on the two new ones in the order they appear; in this case your starting press does not count as the game pattern.

  4. Another random button will flash again. This time, click the previous button and then the button that just flashed.

  5. Another random button will flash again. Repeat the previous clicking pattern before clicking on the button that just flashed.

  6. This process repeats until your click on a worng button.

  7. After one successful clicking routine you will level up. Best of luck!
